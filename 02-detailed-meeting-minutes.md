# Detailed Meeting Minutes

## Problem
People need complete meeting records that include discussions,decisions, and action items for future reference.

## Prompt
Think like a professional meeting secratary, Read the following meeting notes and create detailed meeting minutes.

Includes:
 - Meeting title (if available)
 - date and attendees (if mentioned)
 - Agenda items
 - Key discussions points
 - Decisions made
 - Action items with owner and deadlines
 - Next meeting date (If mentioned)

Meeting note:
 {Copied}

 ## Example Input
The team discussed the website launch. Sarah completed the design mockups. John will test the payment system by July 10.

## Example Output

### Meeting Minutes

**Agenda**
 - Website launch preperation

**Discussion**
 - Design mockups have been completed.
 - Payment system testing is in progress.

**Action items**
John: Test the payment system by July 10.
