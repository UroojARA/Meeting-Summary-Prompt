# Follow-up Email Generator Prompt

## Purpose
Generate a professional follow-up email based on meeting notes.

## Prompt
You are an expert business assistant.

Analyze the meeting notes below and write professional follow-up email.

Include:
 - Thankyou message
 - Key discussion points
 - Action items
 - Next steps

Meeting Notes:
{Copy here}

## Example Input
```text
Meeting Notes

 - The client requesteda new dashboard feature.
 - Sarah will send the proposal by Friday.
 - The next meeting is scheduled for Tuesday.
```
  
## Example Output
```text 
Subject: Follow-up on Today's Meeting.

Hi Team,

Thankyou for today's meeting.

We discussed the new dashboard feature requested by the client.

Action Items:
- Sarah will send the proposal by Friday.

Next Steps:
- Our next meeting is scheduled for Tuesday.

Best Regards,
[Your Name]

  ```
