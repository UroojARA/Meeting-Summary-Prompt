# Meeting Summary Prompt
A collection of AI prompts for generating professional meeting summaries, minutes, and action items.

## Purpose
this repository contain prompts that help users:
 - Summarize meeting discussions.
 - Extract Key Decisions.
 - Identify action items and deadlines.
 - Creating professional meeting minutes.
 - Generate followup emails and reports.

## Prompts Included
 1. General Meeting Summary
 2. Detailed Meeting Minutes
 3. Executive Summary
 4. Action Items Extractor
 5. Client Meeting Summary
 6. Team Standup Summary
 7. Decision Tracker
 8. Followup Email Generator
 9. Bullet-Point Summary
 10. Simple Meeting Explaination
