# Executive Summary 

## Problem
Busy managers and executives need a quick overview of a meeting without reading the entire transcript.

## Prompt
Think like a executive assistant and summarize the following meeting in less than 200 words.

Focus on:
 - Key topics discussed
 - Important decisions
 - Risks or challenges
 - Next steps

Meeting notes
{copied}

## Example Input
The team discussed launching the new webite next month. Sarah will complete the homepage design by Friday. John will test the payment system before July 10. Marketing activities begin on July 15. The team also discussed potential risks and agreed to review progress next week.

## Example Output
The team finalized plans for the website launch. Key actons include completing the homepage design, testing the payment system and start marketing on July 15. the team will review progress next week
