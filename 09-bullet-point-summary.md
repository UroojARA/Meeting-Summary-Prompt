# Bullet Point Summary Prompt

## Purpose
Convert meeting notes into a concise bullet-point summary highlighting the most important information.

## Prompt

You are an expert meeting assistant

Analyze the meeting notes below and create a clear bullet-point summary.

Focus on:
 - Key desicions
 - Action Items
 - Deadlines
 - Next Steps

Meeting Notes:
{copy here}

---

## Example Input
```text
 Today's discussion was mainly about the website project.Everyone liked the new design and agreed to move forward with it. John mentioned that he can finish updating the website by Friday. there was also a quick discusiion about checking the progress again, so the team decided to meet the next Monday.

```

## Example Output
```text
 - The team approved the new website design during today's meeting.
 - John will update the website by Friday.
 - The next meeting is scheduled for Monday.
```
