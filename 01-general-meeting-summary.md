# General Meeting Summary 
People often have long meeting notes or transcripts and need a clear, professional summary of key points, decisions, and action items

## Prompts 
Think like a professional meeting assistant. Read the following meeting notes and create a professional summary.
Your summary should include:
 - Main discussion points
 - Important desicions made
 - Action Items
 - Deadline (If needed)
 - Person responsible for each task(If mentioned)

Meeting notes:
{copied}

## Example Input
We discussed launching the new website next month. Sarah will finish the homepage design by friday. John needs to test the payment system before July 10. Everyone agreed to start marketing on July 15.

## Example Output

### Meeting Summary 

**Main Discussion**
The team discussed launching the new website next month

**Decisions**
 - The website launched was approved.
 - Marketing activities will begin on July 15.

**Action Items**
 - Sarah: Complete the homepage design by Friday.
 - John: Test the payment system before July 10.

**Next Steps**
- Prepare for the website launch and begin marketing activities on July 15.
